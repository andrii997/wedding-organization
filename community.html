Philanthropy
Buffalo Olmsted Park Conservancy
The mission of the Buffalo Olmsted Parks Conservancy, a 501(c)3 non-profit membership organization, is to promote, preserve, restore, enhance and ensure maintenance of Olmsted Parks and Parkways in the Greater Buffalo area to guarantee Olmsted Park experiences for current and future generations.

“Our team is proud to support this effort by participating as volunteers in maintaining the parks beauty and in replanting some of the areas of the historic Delaware Park in the heart of Buffalo.” - Emir Culov
Learn More
Elmwood Village Association
The mission of the Elmwood Village Association is to encourage a balance of growth and preservation, to support community-led projects and to promote an inclusive neighborhood for people to live, work, visit and prosper.

“Elmwood Village is our community and our team is dedicated to helping make it a vibrant and growing part of the Buffalo revival process.” - Amela Culov
